### Pain Point Deep Dives

#### Pain Point #1: App Switching

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Description** | Users must constantly switch between calendar and finance apps to get complete picture |
| **Frequency** | 10-20 times per day for heavy users |
| **Impact** | Cognitive load, lost context, reduced tracking consistency |
| **Current Workarounds** | Split-screen (awkward), manual notes, simply not checking |
| **Life Solution** | Unified dashboard showing calendar + spending together |

**User Quotes:**
- "I check my calendar 15 times a day and my bank app maybe 3. The disconnect is real."
- "By the time I open my finance app, I've forgotten what that $40 charge was for."

#### Pain Point #2: Forgetting to Track

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Description** | Users forget to log expenses, especially small cash transactions |
| **Frequency** | 30-50% of transactions go untracked |
| **Impact** | Inaccurate budgets, end-of-month surprises |
| **Current Workarounds** | Receipt hoarding, batch entry (painful), simply estimating |
| **Life Solution** | Real-time notifications, quick-entry widgets, bank sync |

#### Pain Point #3: Missing Context

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Description** | Finance apps show WHAT was spent but not WHY or in what context |
| **Frequency** | Affects every spending review |
| **Impact** | Can't identify spending triggers, can't optimize |
| **Current Workarounds** | Manual notes in spreadsheets, memory |
| **Life Solution** | Calendar-expense linking shows context |

## Research Methodology

### Proposed User Research Activities

| Method | Timeline | Participants | Goal |
|--------|----------|--------------|------|
| **User Interviews** | Month 1-2 | 10-15 | Deep understanding of workflows |
| **Surveys** | Month 2, 6, 12 | 100+ | Quantify needs and satisfaction |
| **Usability Testing** | Ongoing | 5-10 per feature | Validate designs |
| **Beta Feedback** | Month 3-4 | 50-100 | Pre-launch iteration |
| **App Store Reviews** | Ongoing | All users | Continuous feedback |
| **Analytics** | Ongoing | All users | Behavioral insights |
| **NPS Surveys** | Quarterly | Sample | Loyalty tracking |

### Key Research Questions

1. What is the current workflow for managing calendar and finances?
2. What are the biggest frustrations with existing solutions?
3. How much would users pay for a unified solution?
4. What features are essential vs. nice-to-have?
5. What concerns do users have about privacy and security?
6. How do users discover new productivity apps?
7. What would make users recommend the app to others?
