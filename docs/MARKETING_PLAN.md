### Launch Strategy by Phase

#### Phase 1: Soft Launch (Month 1-2)

| Objective | Establish product-market fit, gather feedback |
|-----------|-----------------------------------------------|
| Target | 5,000-10,000 downloads |
| Geography | US only |
| Channels | Organic, word-of-mouth, Product Hunt |
| Budget | $0-500 |

**Key Activities:**
- TestFlight beta with 100 users
- Iterate based on feedback
- Build initial case studies
- Create content foundation

#### Phase 2: Growth Launch (Month 3-6)

| Objective | Scale user acquisition, optimize conversion |
|-----------|---------------------------------------------|
| Target | 50,000-100,000 downloads |
| Geography | US, Canada, UK, Australia |
| Channels | ASO, paid ads, content marketing |
| Budget | $1,000-3,000/month |

**Key Activities:**
- Apple Search Ads campaigns
- Influencer partnerships
- Content marketing scale-up
- Referral program launch

#### Phase 3: Scale (Month 7-12)

| Objective | Establish market presence, maximize growth |
|-----------|--------------------------------------------|
| Target | 200,000+ downloads |
| Geography | English-speaking markets, Western Europe |
| Channels | All channels, strategic partnerships |
| Budget | $5,000-10,000/month |
