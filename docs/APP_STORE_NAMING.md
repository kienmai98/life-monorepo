# App Store Naming Guide

This document provides naming strategies for App Store Optimization (ASO) for the Life app on iOS App Store and Google Play Store.

---

## App Title Options

### Option Analysis

| Option | iOS (30 chars max) | Android (50 chars max) | Pros | Cons |
|--------|-------------------|------------------------|------|------|
| **Life** | ✅ 4 chars | ✅ 4 chars | Simple, memorable, on-brand | Low search visibility |
| **Life - Calendar & Tasks** | ✅ 22 chars | ✅ 22 chars | Includes key features, better ASO | Slightly generic |
| **Life: Planner & Organizer** | ✅ 26 chars | ✅ 26 chars | Action-oriented keywords | "Planner" is competitive |
| **Life - Your Personal Organizer** | ❌ 32 chars | ✅ 32 chars | Benefit-focused | Too long for iOS |

### Recommended Titles

#### Primary Title (iOS)
```
Life - Calendar & Tasks
```

#### Primary Title (Android)
```
Life - Calendar, Tasks, Notes & Money
```

### Alternative Title Options

#### Option 1: Feature-Focused
```
iOS:    Life - Planner & Calendar
Android: Life - Daily Planner, Calendar & Tasks
```

#### Option 2: Benefit-Focused
```
iOS:    Life - Organize Your Day
Android: Life - Organize Your Day, Tasks & Calendar
```

#### Option 3: Positioning-Focused
```
iOS:    Life - All-in-One Organizer
Android: Life - All-in-One Organizer for Everything
```

## Review Guidelines Compliance

### What to Avoid

❌ **Don't claim:**
- "Best calendar app" (superlative without proof)
- "#1 organizer" (ranking claim)
- "Free" if there are in-app purchases (without clarification)
- Unverified privacy claims

❌ **Don't include:**
- Pricing information in description (can become outdated)
- Platform names in title ("Life for iOS")
- Special characters for attention (!!!, ALL CAPS)

### What to Include

✅ **Required:**
- Accurate feature descriptions
- Privacy policy link
- Terms of service link
- Support contact information

✅ **Recommended:**
- Regular updates based on user feedback
- Seasonal relevance (New Year = organization resolutions)
- Feature highlights that differentiate from competitors
